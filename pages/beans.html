Ingredients:

  • 1 can of beans
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of pepper
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil

Steps:

  1. add oil to pan on medium heat
  2. after oil heats up add salt and pepper to pan
  3. add beans and turn up the heat and stir
  4. turn off the heat, let cool, and eat
